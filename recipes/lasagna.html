World's Best Lasagna!

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella cheese, all baked to perfection. It's a hearty and comforting dish perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and tomato paste to the skillet. Season with salt, pepper, and basil. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta cheese, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  7. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
