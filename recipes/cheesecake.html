Classic Cheesecake

Description

This creamy and rich classic cheesecake features a buttery graham cracker crust and a smooth, tangy filling. Perfect for any celebration or as a decadent dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter. Press into the bottom of the pan to form the crust.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add 1 cup sugar and vanilla; mix until combined.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing on low speed after each addition.
  5. Mix in sour cream, flour, and salt until just combined.
  6. Pour filling over the crust and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 55-65 minutes, until the center is almost set. Turn off oven, crack the door, and let cheesecake cool for 1 hour.
  8. Remove from oven and chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
